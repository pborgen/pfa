# PFA - Premier Fitness Alliance Training App - Project Principles

## Project Vision
PFA is the official mobile training application for **Premier Fitness Alliance**, empowering coaches to deliver precision, biomechanics-based training programs to female soccer athletes. The app enables coaches to create and manage customized workout programs while athletes track their performance and progress toward elite-level athletic development.

## About Premier Fitness Alliance
Premier Fitness Alliance is a performance training organization based in Wethersfield, Connecticut, specializing in athletic development for female soccer players. Their mission: "Empower the next generation of female athletes by harmonizing elite science with authentic mentorship."

**Core Values:**
- Unapologetic authenticity
- Precision training using biomechanics
- Protection and development of young athletes
- Authentic mentorship and support

## Core Principles

### 1. Coach-Centric Design
- Prioritize the coach's workflow for creating and managing training programs
- Make it easy to customize workouts for individual athlete needs
- Allow coaches to track athlete progress, adherence, and performance
- Support biomechanics-based training methodology

### 2. Athlete Empowerment
- Provide athletes with clear targets and expectations
- Enable detailed performance tracking (planned vs actual)
- Show progress over time to build confidence
- Support athlete development through data and feedback

### 3. Simple and Intuitive
- Clean, minimal interface focused on core functionality
- Easy navigation between athletes and workouts
- Quick workout creation and editing
- Mobile-optimized for use during training sessions

### 4. Performance-First
- Optimized for iOS devices (primary platform)
- Offline-capable for gym/field environments with poor connectivity
- Fast loading and responsive interactions
- Real-time data saving to prevent loss

### 5. Athletic Specificity
- Support various training types (strength, speed, agility, conditioning, flexibility)
- Soccer-specific exercise library
- Customizable parameters (sets, reps, weight, duration, rest)
- Video demonstrations for proper form and biomechanics

### 6. Professional & Secure
- Reliable data persistence and cloud backup
- Professional appearance suitable for elite athlete training
- Secure athlete data handling (FERPA/privacy compliant)
- Coach-athlete confidentiality

## Success Criteria
- Coaches can create a customized training session in under 2 minutes
- Athletes can easily view and log their assigned workouts
- Athletes see clear progress indicators and performance trends
- Data is reliably saved and accessible across sessions
- App performs smoothly with 100+ athletes and 500+ workouts
- 90%+ athlete adherence to assigned training programs
